🔵 HBC Account Closure Retention Flowchart

Step 1: Identify the Reason for Closure

"I am so sorry to hear that you want to cancel your Hudson’s Bay credit card! May I ask what’s leading you to close your HBC account today?"

Step 2: General Request to Close

💡 Highlight the value of their existing HBC card.

"I completely understand! Your card gives you 2% cashback on HBC store purchases and 1% everywhere else!"

Step 3: Concerned About Losing HBC Rewards

💡 Reassure the customer that their in-store purchases still earn rewards.

"Your in-store purchases continue earning 2% cashback!"

Step 4: Not Using the Card

💡 Engage the customer to understand their spending habits.

"I totally get that! Just out of curiosity, what’s stopped you from using your HBC card?"

Step 5: Exploring Neo Card Swaps

💡 Position a Neo card based on the customer’s spending habits.

"At Neo, we offer credit cards tailored to your needs! Would you like a better fit?"
